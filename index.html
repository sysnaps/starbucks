About Coffee Mood Finder

Welcome to Starbucks Coffee Mood Finder, where we believe your coffee should match your life situation!

The Story

We started with a simple idea: what if your coffee could be perfectly tailored to what's actually happening in your life? Instead of just choosing between light or dark roast, what if you could choose a coffee based on whether you just got promoted, survived a Monday meeting marathon, or need to cope with kids?

How It Works

Our quiz analyzes your current life scenario, preferences, and vibe to recommend the perfect coffee from our menu. Each of our "scenario coffees" is crafted with specific ingredients and vibes to complement different moments.
